Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and let you manage your financial records. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality of Spendly.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us through the app.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to run the app, but these providers do not have access to your expense data beyond what is necessary to operate the servers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app. We take privacy seriously and will respond promptly to any concerns.
